Exam practice tip: For each past paper, follow this proven cycle:

✓ Take the exam paper under timed conditions (no peeking at answers!)
✓ Check your answers using the Answer Booklet and Mark Scheme
✓ Record your average mark in the "Mark" column (add up your scores and divide by total marks)
✓ Review mistakes and revisit weak topics in the LGCSE Mathematics Guide

Only check "Completed" when you have honestly attempted the paper AND reviewed your errors. Repeat papers after 2 weeks to measure improvement.
