Understanding the Lesotho Education Pathway
Students in Lesotho typically follow this mathematics progression:
PSLE (Grade 7) → LGCSE (Grade 8 - 11) → Post-Secondary
Most Lesotho secondary schools follow the LGCSE pathway, which is the national curriculum aligned with local educational policies. The Examinations Council of Lesotho (ECoL) administers all national examinations.

Lesotho mathematics curricula information is updated regularly to reflect current educational policies.
For official curriculum documents, please refer to the Examinations Council of Lesotho (ECoL) or Cambridge International (for IGCSE).
